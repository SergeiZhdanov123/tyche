================================================================================
                              WHAT IS ERNS?
================================================================================

Erns is a professional-grade earnings intelligence platform built for traders, 
investors, and analysts who need fast, accurate, and actionable financial data. 
It transforms the way users interact with earnings season by combining real-time 
data scraping, AI-powered analysis, and a sleek terminal-style interface into one 
powerful tool.

At its core, Erns aggregates earnings data from multiple sources — Yahoo Finance, 
Nasdaq, SEC filings, and more — and presents it through an intuitive dashboard 
that makes complex financial data instantly digestible. Whether you're tracking 
upcoming earnings dates, analyzing post-earnings price reactions, or screening 
for stocks that beat or missed EPS estimates, Erns puts all that information at 
your fingertips.

KEY FEATURES:

• Earnings Screener — Filter upcoming earnings by date, ticker, BMO/AMC timing, 
  EPS estimates, revenue forecasts, and market cap. Find the plays before the 
  market moves.

• Earnings Surprises — Instantly see which companies beat or missed analyst 
  expectations, with surprise percentages, actual vs. estimated EPS, and 
  historical patterns.

• AI-Powered Signals — AI analyzes your watchlist and generates bullish, bearish, 
  or neutral trading signals with confidence scores, key technical levels, 
  catalysts, and risk assessments.

• Deep Analysis — Click any signal for an AI-generated deep dive: current 
  positioning, recent earnings breakdown, technical setup, upcoming catalysts, 
  risk factors, and specific trade setups with entry/exit levels.

• Erns Chat — An intelligent AI assistant specialized in earnings, SEC filings, 
  financial health, and market reactions. Ask it anything about a company's 
  fundamentals and get data-driven answers.

• Sentiment Analysis — AI-driven sentiment scoring on SEC filings (10-Q, 10-K, 
  8-K), determining whether a company's language signals bullish, bearish, or 
  neutral outlook.

• Interactive Charts — Candlestick and line charts with multiple timeframes (1D, 
  1W, 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y) and granularity controls (1min, 5min, 1H, 1D, 1W).

• Live News Feed — Real-time financial news aggregated from Reuters, CNBC, 
  Bloomberg, Yahoo Finance, and more, with sentiment tagging on every headline.

• Watchlist Sync — Build and manage your watchlist, synced across all features. 
  Your signals, screener, and dashboard adapt to the tickers you care about.

• Public API — Full REST API with documentation and an interactive playground. 
  Developers and quants can integrate Erns data directly into their own tools, 
  bots, and trading systems.

• Terminal Aesthetic — A dark, premium interface inspired by Bloomberg Terminal 
  and trading desks. Designed for speed, information density, and zero 
  distractions.

Erns is built for people who take earnings seriously. It's not a toy — it's a 
weapon for earnings season.
